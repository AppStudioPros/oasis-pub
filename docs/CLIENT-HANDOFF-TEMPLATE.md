### What to Do If Something Looks Wrong

**If an edit isn't showing on the site:**
Wait 60 seconds, then do a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac). If it still hasn't updated after 2 minutes, contact us.

**If you're not receiving contact form emails:**
Check your Messages inbox in the admin panel — the message will be there even if the email had an issue. Contact us and we'll investigate.

**If you can't log in:**
Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page. If that doesn't work, contact us and we'll reset it.

**If something on the website looks broken:**
Take a screenshot and send it to us. Include what page you were on and what device you were using.

## Notes for Kelsi When Filling This Out

- **Be specific about what they CAN'T edit** — if they don't have a food menu, say "drinks menu only." Prevents confusion.
- **Include screenshots** — a screenshot of the menu editor with arrows is worth 500 words.
- **List the destination email(s)** for form submissions so they know where to look.
- **Send this BEFORE launch day** so they have time to read it and ask questions.
- **Follow up 3-5 days after launch** — ask if they have questions and if everything is working as expected. This one touchpoint prevents most "it's broken" emails.
